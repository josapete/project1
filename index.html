Free Clothing

If you wish to recieve free clothing per month you would need to provide legal documentation of your current state of well being. If you are of the age 65 or older or cant work due to a handicap we will provide clothing alond with other necessities that will aid you in your daily life. If your a single mother with children and cant obtain a jobe your will recieve clothing for yourself once a month and clothing for your children twice a month. If you currently recieve aid from Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) your are able to recieve free clothing once a month. If you are recieving govermnet aid you will be legible to recieve free clothing from us as well.

When you apply for free clothing you must wait a few business days for it to process. If you are accepted you will recieve your free clothing next month on the day you applied. When you recieve your clothing you will be given the necessities. This includes undergarments, pants, shirts, socks, and 2 pairs of shoes. If your shoes were to be damaged to the point of being unable to wear them just simply place them in a package like container and send them back to us. We will send you a new pair to replace your damages pair. If you need business clothing for a job or interview you will need to provide a few details for us. The first being the name of the company your are applying for, the address of the buidling and store number if possible, and lastly proof of the interview or job acceptance.
